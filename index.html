The Office TV Show Questionaire

This is a 5 question game where you will be asked numerous questions about The Office, an NBC mockumentary sitcom television series. You will have a total of 60 seconds to complete this quiz. If you answer a question wrong, you will be deducted 10 seconds from the remaining time. Once you are done you can input your initials to log your Highscore.
